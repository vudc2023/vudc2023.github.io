High School Finals

  • 1
    TH, as the couple, would not search for their biological child
    Info Slide

    Your husband took a paternity test with your 10-year-old child. After showing him the negative results, he accused you of cheating. You told him to take the tests together and as it turns out, they are not your child either. You learned that your child had been swapped at the hospital 10 years ago.

  • 2
    THP a world where children are adopted through closed adoptions
    Info Slide

    A closed adoption is one where no identifying information about the birth family or the adoptive family is shared between the two, and there is no contact between the families. An open adoption allows for some association among the birth parents, adoptive parents, and the child they adopted.

  • 3
    THW permanently delete the criminal history of children in conflict with the law, after they have served their sentence
    Info Slide

    "Children in conflict with the law" or CICLs refers to individuals who are below the age of majority (typically 18 years old) and have been involved in criminal activities or are accused of committing offenses that are considered illegal under the legal framework of a particular jurisdiction.

    TH, as the anti-AI art movement, would aggressively generate AI art to oversaturate the datasets
    Info Slide

    AI art generators work by using machine learning algorithms to create new artwork. This is when a large dataset of existing paintings, drawings, and other art are used to help the program differentiate between different art styles and techniques. Recently, programs have begun referencing existing AI art to generate new AI art which has yielded worse results.

  • 1
    THS optimizing the educational curricula of college majors with professional licensure exams towards passing those exams (e.g. removing general education subjects perceived as unnecessary)
  • 2
    THW abolish the autonomous and deregulated statuses for HEIs
    Info Slide

    The Commission on Higher Education (CHED) grants "autonomous" and "deregulated" statuses to certain Higher Education Institutions (HEIs). These statuses have benefits that include freedom from regular monitoring from CHED, priority in granting of subsidies, freedom to offer new programs and put up branches or satellite campuses without having to secure permits, and privilege in determining their curricular programs. Deregulated HEIs must still secure permits for new programs and campuses.

    • Round 2

  • 1
    THR the expansion of aquaculture in developing countries
    Info Slide

    Aquaculture, also known as fish farming, involves raising aquatic organisms in controlled environments. It meets the global demand for seafood, enhances food security, and supports economic development. However, its expansion can harm marine ecosystems by causing habitat loss and increasing the vulnerability of coastal communities to disasters.

  • 2
    THBT environmental activists must boycott and actively denounce the COP27
    Info Slide

    Every year, world leaders gather to discuss climate change at COP, which stands for Conference of the Parties. Since 1995, the United Nations has brought together the 197 signatories to the UN Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCC) to work on how to tackle climate change.

    Insurrection refers to a violent uprising or rebellion against authority, often aiming to overthrow the government or disrupt societal order.
